Welcome to Paris

Eiffel Tower

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, an iconic symbol of Paris, stands tall at 1,063 feet. Known for its breathtaking views and rich history, it features observation decks, a Michelin-starred restaurant, and a champagne bar.

Visitors can enjoy its dazzling lights at night and explore the history behind its construction during the 1889 World’s Fair.

Lourve Museum

Louvre Museum

As the world's largest art museum, the Louvre is home to the famous Mona Lisa and thousands of masterpieces. The museum’s glass pyramid entrance is as iconic as the treasures it houses.

Stroll through its vast halls to witness art from ancient civilizations to the 19th century.

Note:

  • Standard Entry: €17 per person
  • Guided Tour with Skip-the-Line Access: €50 per person
  • Private Night Tour: €120 per person

Arc de Triomphe

Arc de Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe honors those who fought for France. Standing at the top of the Champs-Élysées, it offers a panoramic view of Paris.

The Tomb of the Unknown Soldier beneath it symbolizes the nation’s respect for those lost in wars.

Note:

  • General Entry: €13 per person
  • Rooftop Access with Panoramic View: €18 per person
  • Private Guided Tour: €60 per person

Seine River

Seine River

The Seine River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, flows gracefully through Paris. Take a scenic cruise to see landmarks like Notre Dame and the Eiffel Tower.

Its bridges and riverbanks create a romantic atmosphere, especially at sunset.

  • Private Luxury Yacht Experience: €250 per person
