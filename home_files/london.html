Welcome to London

Big Ben

Big Ben

The iconic Big Ben clock tower, officially known as the Elizabeth Tower, is a symbol of London. It stands proudly next to the Houses of Parliament and chimes every hour, attracting millions of visitors annually.

Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the structure from Westminster Bridge or explore the surrounding historical landmarks.

Note:

  • Big Ben Guided Tour: £20 per person
  • Big Ben and Parliament Full-Day Tour: £45 per person
  • Evening Photography Tour: £30 per person

London Eye

London Eye

The London Eye is one of the world’s tallest observation wheels, offering unparalleled views of the London skyline. Each rotation takes about 30 minutes, providing ample time to capture the beauty of landmarks like the Thames River and St. Paul’s Cathedral.

Experience a sunset ride for a magical view or take a private pod for a unique adventure.

Note:

  • Standard Entry: £25 per person
  • Fast Track Entry: £37 per person
  • Private Pod Experience: £450 (up to 10 people)

Tower Bridge

Tower Bridge

Tower Bridge is an engineering marvel and one of the most photographed landmarks in London. Visitors can explore the glass walkways at the top of the bridge and learn about its fascinating history in the exhibition rooms.

Don’t miss the panoramic views of the city and the chance to witness the bridge lift to allow ships to pass.

Note:

  • General Entry: £12 per person
  • Glass Walkway Experience: £18 per person
  • Private Guided Tour: £50 per person

Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace is the official residence of the British monarch. Visitors can witness the Changing of the Guard ceremony or take a guided tour through the lavish State Rooms during the summer opening.

The surrounding gardens and statues make it a serene location for history enthusiasts and photographers alike.

Note:

  • State Rooms Tour: £25 per person
  • Garden Highlights Tour: £15 per person
  • Changing of the Guard Viewing Experience: £10 per person
